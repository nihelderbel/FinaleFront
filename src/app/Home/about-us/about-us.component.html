À propos de nous

LEONI is a global provider of products, solutions and services for energy and data management in the automotive industry. The group of companies has around 95,000 employees in 27 countries and generated consolidated sales of EUR 5.1 billion in 2022. The value chain ranges from standardized cables and special and data cables to highly complex wiring systems and related components, from development to production. LEONI supports its customers as an innovation partner and solution provider with distinctive development and system expertise on the way to increasingly sustainable and connected mobility concepts. The development of next-generation cable solutions and wiring systems enables for example through zonal architecture reduced complexity and higher levels of automation. LEONI concentrates its research and development work on the automotive industry’s major trends such as electromobility.
